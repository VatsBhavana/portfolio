Organization Skill

Organization skill are abilities to manage time effectively,prioritize tasks,set goals and develop system for achieving those goals.

problem_solving Skill

Define the problem,Analyse the problem, Evaluate the options,Select the best option, Implement the solution

Flexibility Skill

The capacity to adjust to short-term change quickly and calmly so that you can deal with unexpected problems or tasks effectively.

Communication Skill

Communication is the sending and receiving of information and can be one-on-one or between groups of people and can be face-to-face

Creativity Skill

Creativity skills are the ability to think differently generate new ideas and solve problems in innovative ways.

Teamwork Skill

Working cooperatively contributing to groups with ideas, suggestions and effort.clearly define roles and responsibilities for every team member.
